Plan Your Heist

"Plan Your Heist" is a console application that allows users to plan and simulate a heist by assembling a team of skilled members and evaluating their chances of success. Adventurer's Quest is a text-based adventure game where the player takes on the role of an adventurer and completes challenges to prove their awesomeness. The game allows the player to customize their adventurer's name, robe colors, robe length, and hat shininess level. The adventurer will face a series of randomly selected challenges and earn points based on their performance. It utilizes C#.
